How to make Tofu Shawarma

Something Savory: Tofu Shawarma

  1. You will need:
    • 1 block of firm tofu
    • 1 cup of rice
    • 4-5 cloves of garlic
    • 4 tbsp of turmeric
    • 4 tbsp of paprika
    • 4 tbsp of cumin
    • salt to taste
    • 6 tbsp of olive oil
    • pepper to taste
    • 1 cup of yogurt
    • 1 cucumber shredded
    • 1 tomato diced
    • 2 stems of dill
  2. Instructions
    • Drain the tofu and wrap it in paper towels to let it dry
    • Dice up tofu into cubes and put into a mixing bowl
    • Mince the garlic cloves
    • To the mixing bowl with tofu, add in the garlic, 2 tbsp turmeric, 4 tbsp paprika, 2 tbsp cumin, 1/2 cup yogurt. Mix it while adding salt and pepper to taste
    • Heat up a flat pan with 3 tbsp olive oil
    • Add in the spiced tofu to the pan on medium heat. Cook till it is crispy and brown on the outside.
    • In the meantime to a rice cooker, add in your rice with 2 tbsp turmeric, 2 tbsp cumin, 2 tbsp olive oil, and salt
    • Add your water and let it cook in the rice cooker till it is done
    • Chop up your dill and tomoatoes. Shred up the cucumber
    • To the 1/2 cup of yogurt that is left, add in your shredded cucumber, chopped dill, salt, and tbsp of olive oil. Mix. Now you have made your tzakiki!
    • Assemeble your bowl with the turmeric rice, tofu shawarma meat, tzakiki and diced tomatoes.
    • Enjoy it with some lemonade
