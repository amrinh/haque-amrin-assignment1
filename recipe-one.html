How to make Tofu Shawarma

You will need:

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic
  • 4 tbsp of turmeric
  • 4 tbsp of paprika
  • 4 tbsp of cumin
  • salt to taste
  • 6 tbsp of olive oil
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of yogurt
  • 1 cucumber shredded
  • 1 tomato diced
  • 2 stems of dill

Instructions

  1. Drain the tofu and wrap it in paper towels to let it dry
  2. Dice up tofu into cubes and put into a mixing bowl
  3. Mince the garlic cloves
  4. To the mixing bowl with tofu, add in the garlic, 2 tbsp turmeric, 4 tbsp paprika, 2 tbsp cumin, 1/2 cup yogurt. Mix it while adding salt and pepper to taste
  5. Heat up a flat pan with 3 tbsp olive oil
  6. Add in the spiced tofu to the pan on medium heat. Cook till it is crispy and brown on the outside.
  7. In the meantime to a rice cooker, add in your rice with 2 tbsp turmeric, 2 tbsp cumin, 2 tbsp olive oil, and salt
  8. Add your water and let it cook in the rice cooker till it is done
  9. Chop up your dill and tomoatoes. Shred up the cucumber
  10. To the 1/2 cup of yogurt that is left, add in your shredded cucumber, chopped dill, salt, and tbsp of olive oil. Mix. Now you have made your tzakiki!

Assembling your bowl

Get your bowl
Put down the rice
Add the tofu on one side
Diced tomotoes on the other side
Drizzle with some tzakiki
Enjoy it with some lemonade
