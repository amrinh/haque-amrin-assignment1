FOR THE FOODIE

Who am I?

Hey guys! My name is Amrin. I started cooking around the age of 6 years old. When I was younger, my dream was to become a chef and have my own Food Network show. That ended up not happening but it did not stop me from cooking.

For the past 8 years, I switched to a plant-based diet and have been innovating new ways to make dishes as delicious from when I wasn't vegan. AKA: no boring salads, green wrap... you get what i am saying.

What is this about?

This blog features some of my favoriteeee dishes that are very easy to make (for the most part). I try my best to keep the recipes simple and effective since I am a college student after all! And a little bit on the lazy side

I hope you will be able to recreate and make these dishes in your own and special way. The more twists the better! Make it so that it has your love and your vision.
